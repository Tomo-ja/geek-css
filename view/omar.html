Font Family elements

The font-style property is mostly used to specify italic text.

This property has three values:
font-style: normal; - The text is shown normally

font-style: italic - The text is shown in italics

font-style: oblique - The text is "leaning" (oblique is very similar to italic, but less supported)

The font-weight property specifies the weight of a font:

This property has two values:
font-weigth: bold; - The text is shown bolded

font-weight: normal; - The text is shown normally

The font-Variant property specifies the weight of a font:

This property has two values:
font-variant: normal; - The text is shown normal

font-variant: small-caps; - The text is shown with small caps
